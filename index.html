Welcome to my Github!
This is the attempted home of my Twine game

For this game, I wanted to try and convey the
feelings of being alone at home and not having
much to do, or having much you want to do. I tried
to accomplish this by giving the player a lot of
looping options to chose from, many of which result
in a wasted day where they feel bad. As I was wokring,
however, I started having a lot of fun, and I added
in a few extra endings and routes that act as fun
little secrets for those who find them
